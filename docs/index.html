ACM India Summer School 2025

AI for Social Good • 11-Day Intensive Program
Hosted by IIT Gandhinagar • June 2-13, 2025

Apply Now | View Schedule

Program Overview

Join us for an intensive 11-day program exploring cutting-edge AI techniques applied to social good domains including healthcare, sustainability, and agriculture.

Our program combines expert talks, hands-on tutorials, and interactive panel discussions to foster both technical expertise and interdisciplinary collaboration.
