ACM India Summer School 2025: AI for Social Good

ACM India Summer School 2025

Hosted by IIT Gandhinagar, Gandhinagar
📅 02–13 June 2025

Overview

The ACM Summer School on AI for Social Good at IIT Gandhinagar offers an intensive 11-day program on AI techniques applied to social good domains such as healthcare, sustainability, and agriculture.

The school will feature expert talks, hands-on tutorials, and panel discussions, fostering both technical expertise and interdisciplinary collaboration.

📌 Applying for the Summer Schools

The application fee for this school program is ₹150 + 18% GST.
You can give first and second preference in the application form.
All payments are non-refundable. Please apply after careful consideration since demand is high and seats are limited.

📝 Selection & Registration

  • Selection Based on data submitted in the application. Selected students will be notified by email.
  • Registration Only selected students must pay non-refundable fees (₹3000 + 18% GST) which includes lodging and boarding at the venue.

    • Once selected to a school, you cannot switch to another.
    • Ensure you pay and register to be fair to those not selected.
